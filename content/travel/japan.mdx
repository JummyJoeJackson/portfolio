Notes and photos from my two-week journey through Japan, from the neon streets of Tokyo to the quiet temples of Kyoto.

---

## Highlights

- The massive scale of Tokyo and seemingly endless city
- Taking a bullet train to Kyoto
- The quiet remorseful atmosphere of Hiroshima
- I felt right at home in Osaka with the friendly people and similar vibe to Toronto

---

## Lowlights

- It was extremely hot and humid
- We walked around 10km a day
