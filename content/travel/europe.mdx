Revisiting some places I've been to before, but with a new perspective.

## Paris

I've been to Paris before, but staying in an Airbnb pushed me to explore the city in a way I hadn't before and appreciate its beauty. I visited the Louvre, the Palace of Versailles, and also celebrated my sister's birthday atop the Eiffel Tower.

## London

Took the Eurostar to London. London was new to me, but we were too short on time to explore it as much as I would have liked. I visited the London Bridge, the Big Ben, and the Palace of Westminster.

## Edinburgh

A passing hurricane cancelled our train to Edinburgh, which almost cost us the trip. We ended up taking a plane, and I'm glad we did. Edinburgh was a city I had never been to before, and I was blown away by its beauty. I was staying near the Edinburgh Castle and I could see it from everywhere.
