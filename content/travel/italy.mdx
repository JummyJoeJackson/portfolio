Notes and photos from my trip to Italy, from the ancient ruins of Rome to the beautiful Amalfi Coast.

---

## Rome

The colosseum is massive. And the vatican is breathtaking. However, the big city vibe was not what I was looking for.

---

## Florence

Took a bullet train to Florence. Miles of art and history. The rain was a bit of a bummer, but the city was still beautiful.

---

## Naples & Pompeii

My favorite city in Italy. The food was amazing and the people were friendly. Beautiful views of the ocean and Vesuvius. Walked throught the ancient ruins of Pompeii and felt the simplicity of life back then.

---

## Amalfi Coast

Hours of seemingly impossible roads, but the views were worth it. Stopped in Positano and ate 1 cent bread along with an orange. Views of crystal blue water were so good that even actor Jesse Williams was there.
