Exploring the Pyrenees and the Basque Country.

---

## Paris

Paris was beautiful, went with my school. Met some friends I'm still close with today. The food was not to my taste.

---

## The Pyrenees

The mountains were beautiful, and stick out from the landscape in a way I've never seen before. A sole monastery sat on top of a mountain, the views were incredible.

---

## Barcelona

I thought I spoke spanish, but I was wrong. I couldn't understand a word anyone was saying. From park Guell to the Sagrada Familia, the architecture was stunning and the food was amazing.
